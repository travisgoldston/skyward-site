Case Study

Budgetocity

Strategic organic growth and conversion design that moved the needle for a budget-planning SaaS.

Focus: SEO · Conversion Design

Book a Strategy Call Explore services

The challenge

Traffic was flat. Sign-ups weren’t converting.

Budgetocity had a solid product for budget-conscious consumers and small businesses, but organic visibility was limited and the sign-up flow had too much friction. We needed to grow qualified traffic and improve conversion without a major product overhaul.

Goals: increase organic sessions, improve rankings for high-intent keywords, and lift free-trial conversion through clearer value proposition and streamlined UX.

Approach

SEO: Owning the “budget” space

We ran a full content and technical audit, then built a topical cluster around budget planning, savings tools, and comparison content. On-page optimization and internal linking tied it all together so Budgetocity could rank for both informational and commercial-intent queries.

Conversion design: Less friction, clearer value

We redesigned the landing and sign-up flow: clearer headlines, social proof above the fold, and a simplified form. A/B tests on CTA copy and layout led to a new default that we then rolled out site-wide.

Coverage expansion

Within 12 months, Budgetocity saw a substantial lift in organic traffic and sign-ups. The combination of targeted SEO and conversion-focused design made the biggest difference on high-intent pages.
